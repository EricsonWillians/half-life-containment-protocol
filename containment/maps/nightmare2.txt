INSTALLATION:

1. 	Run Winzip and unzip nightmare2.zip.

2. 	Open the nightmare2 folder and place the nightmare2.bsp, 				nightmare2.res, and nightmare2.tx in your valve/maps folder.  

3.	Place the 6 (six) grave256_.tga files into your valve/gfx/env folder.

4.	Put the wavs in a folder called nite2 and put it in the valve/sound folder.

5.	Place the nite2 folder (containing map models) inside the valve/models folder.

6. 	Place the fixflame.spr in your valve/sprites folder.
